❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Do I need to modify any files?
A: No! Everything works out of the box.

Q: Can I change the port?
A: Yes! Edit src/pm_tool_backend.py and search for "port=3000"

Q: Can I persist data?
A: Currently stores in memory. Add a database in pm_tool_backend.py

Q: Does it work on Windows?
A: Yes! Use venv\Scripts\activate instead of source venv/bin/activate

Q: Is it secure?
A: For local use. Add authentication for production.

Q: Can I integrate with my real PM tool?
A: Yes! Update PM_TOOL_API in .env to point to your tool's API

Q: What if the port is already in use?
A: Use 'lsof -i :3000' (Mac/Linux) or 'netstat -ano | findstr :3000' (Windows)
   Then kill the process and restart.
